## Dataset Preparation

We collected images for two personalities: Ryan Reynolds and Justin Bieber.

For each person, 25 images were collected from online sources such as Google Images and public websites.

The images were selected carefully to ensure:
- Clear face visibility
- Different angles, lighting conditions, and expressions
- No duplicate or blurry images

The dataset was split into:
- Training set: 20 images per person
- Validation set: 5 images per person

All images were resized to 224x224 pixels during preprocessing.

The dataset was organized into separate folders for each personality to enable efficient training using ImageDataGenerator.
